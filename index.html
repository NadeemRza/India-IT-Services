Welcome To India IT Services

Founded in 2009, India IT Services (IITS)is a home grown company. It was started as a web hosting company and later entered into custom application development (desktop and web), mobile application development, product development and very recently into IT man power supply.

IITS: Driven by a solutions mindset

We're driven by a passion to help our clients build stronger, more agile and more innovative businesses.

IITS enables enterprises to make their current operations as efficient and cost-effective as possible, and to invest in innovation to unleash new potential across their organizations.

What makes IITS unique is our ability to help clients meet both challenges. We help them enhance productivity by ensuring that vital business functions work faster, cheaper and better. And, our ability to conceptualize, architect and implement new and expanded capabilities allows clients to transform legacy models to take their business to the next level.
